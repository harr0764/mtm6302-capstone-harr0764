Bulbasaur

Seed Pokémon

Bulbasaur is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves into Ivysaur starting at level 16, which evolves into Venusaur starting at level 32. Along with Charmander and Squirtle, Bulbasaur is one of the three first partner Pokémon of Kanto available at the beginning of Pokémon Red, Green, Blue, FireRed, and LeafGreen.

Description

Bulbasaur is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon. It has the ability to absorb sunlight and use it to grow and thrive.
